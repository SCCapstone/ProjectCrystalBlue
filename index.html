Introduction

Project Crystal Blue (PCB) is a Mac OSX and iOS application for geologists looking for a simple system to catalog, track, and analyze their geologic samples and data. PCB can be run on a single machine or synchronized across multiple computers and iOS devices. PCB handles collecting samples in the field, managing large collections, and performing procedures in the lab. Adding existing collections is a breeze with csv importing and exporting features.

Collecting Your Samples In The Field

Project Crystal Blue makes it easy to add new sources you have collected in the field with an easy, step-by-step walkthrough on the iOS application. The walkthrough prompts you for specific information such as rock type, lithology, age method, etc. Additionally, PCB prefills GPS coordinates and other metadata so you do not have to. The walkthrough concludes with the ability to add related images directly from the camera or from stored images in your iOS device's gallery.

Synchronizing With The Cloud

After collecting sources in the field, you can easily synchronize your data across multiple computers and iOS devices. Because PCB supports offline activity, you can use both the OSX and iOS clients without Internet connection. After a connection has been established, your data can safely be stored in the cloud with a simple click. If multiple devices are working with your collection, PCB will intelligently determine how to handle inconsistencies in the data and update your collection accordingly.
